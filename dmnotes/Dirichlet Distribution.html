Dirichlet Distribution

It is a multivariate generalization of the beta distribution. A k-dimensional Dirichlet random variable x can take values in the (k−1)-simplex (a k-vector x lies in the (k−1)-simplex if xi≥0, ∑ki=1xi=1).

PDF:

f(x1,⋯,xK;α1,⋯,αK)=1B(α)∏i=1Kxαi−1i

where the parameter α is a k-vector with components αi>0 and the multivariate Beta function acts as the normalizing constant:

B(α)=∏Ki=1Γ(αi)Γ(∑Ki=1αi)

It's the conjugate prior of the multinomial distribution.

Properties:

Var(xi)=αi(∑Kk=1αk−αi)(∑Kk=1αk)2(∑Kk=1αk+1)
